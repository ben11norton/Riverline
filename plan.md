# Product Portfolio Overview

## Purpose

The business consists of two independent software products that address different segments of the environmental risk assessment market. While they share similar datasets and environmental expertise, they are designed with different technical architectures, customer bases, and revenue models.

---

# Product 1 – Riverline

## Overview

Riverline is a lightweight web-based environmental screening platform designed to provide rapid site assessments through an intuitive user interface.

The objective is to enable users to quickly evaluate environmental constraints before committing to more detailed investigations.

## Core Features

* Address and coordinate search
* Interactive mapping interface
* Flood risk screening
* River proximity analysis
* Elevation and terrain information
* Environmental constraint summaries
* Downloadable reports
* AI-generated mitigation recommendations (future)

## Target Customers

* Property developers
* Architects
* Estate agents
* Landowners
* Farmers
* Small engineering consultancies
* Environmental consultants requiring rapid preliminary screening

## Business Model

Riverline is intended to operate as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform through a combination of:

* Subscription plans
* Pay-per-report pricing
* Premium analytical features

The focus is high user volume with relatively low operating costs.

---

# Product 2 – Flood Risk Screening Tool

## Overview

The Flood Risk Screening Tool is a professional desktop/web platform developed using ASP.NET MVC and Three.js.

Unlike Riverline, this platform performs advanced environmental analysis and visualisation for engineering and planning applications.

It is intended to support professional decision-making rather than simple screening.

## Core Features

* 3D terrain generation
* Digital Elevation Model (DEM) processing
* High-resolution satellite imagery
* River network visualisation
* Flood modelling and pooling simulation
* Risk scoring algorithms
* Automated mitigation planning
* Professional reporting outputs

## Target Customers

* Environmental consultancies
* Civil engineering firms
* Renewable energy developers
* Municipal authorities
* Mining companies
* Infrastructure planners
* Insurance and risk assessment organisations

## Business Model

Revenue is generated through premium professional services, including:

* Per-project flood assessments
* Enterprise software licensing
* Consulting engagements
* Annual support and maintenance contracts
* Custom environmental modelling projects

The platform is designed for lower user volume but significantly higher project value.

---

# Relationship Between the Products

Although both products utilise similar environmental datasets, they serve different purposes.

| Riverline                          | Flood Risk Screening Tool           |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Lightweight SaaS application       | Professional engineering platform   |
| Rapid screening                    | Detailed environmental modelling    |
| High-volume usage                  | Specialist project work             |
| Subscription and report revenue    | Enterprise licensing and consulting |
| General environmental intelligence | Advanced flood risk assessment      |

Riverline may generate leads for the Flood Risk Screening Tool where detailed analysis is required. However, each product is intended to be commercially sustainable as an independent offering.

---

# Strategic Positioning

The business strategy is based on maintaining two complementary products:

* **Riverline** provides scalable, accessible environmental intelligence for a broad customer base.
* **Flood Risk Screening Tool** delivers advanced analytical capability for organisations requiring detailed technical assessments.

This dual-product approach enables the business to participate in both the SaaS market and the professional environmental consulting software market while leveraging a shared technical foundation and environmental expertise.
