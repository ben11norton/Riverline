Key Takeaway

The tech demo is strong — 3D landscape generation, satellite overlay, flood risk gradient, and automated mitigation planning is genuinely impressive and useful. The gap is not the product — it's the business model. A free tool with a single third-party upsell won't cover its own costs. The screening itself must generate revenue — either per-use, per-subscription, or through regulatory-compliant reporting that consultants will pay for.
